Research Study AON06 - Mollie



By clicking the button below, you will be acting as a customer who has put item x and/or y in their shopping cart and decides to continue to checkout and eventually payment.

This button will first contact the Mollie API to create a payment

On this call, we specify the amount and the currency aswell as a description of the payment. The description is most likely not neccesary.

We also specify a redirectUrl and webhookUrl

The redirectUrl will be used to send you back to our site after successfull payment.

The webhookUrl is used by mollie to send us live updates on your payment process, this gives us insights on when an important step is completed/started by you.

The webhookUrl is not used in this example, but it is a good practice to implement it.

After we successfully receive the payment link, we will redirect you there so that you can pay.
